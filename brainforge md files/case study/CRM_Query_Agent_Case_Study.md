## Challenge
[START OF SECTION]
There was no easy way to answer questions like:
- Who attended?
- Who referred whom?
- Who needs a nudge?

Manual spreadsheet checks made it slow to follow up, while referral gaps went unnoticed and opportunity windows closed. The lack of real-time visibility into engagement data meant the team was always reactive rather than proactive, missing critical moments to nurture leads and acknowledge referral contributors.
